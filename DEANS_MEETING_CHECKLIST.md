## 2. THE "DEFENSE" (What he will grill you on)
Be ready to answer these immediately. Do not hesitate.

**Q: "What happens when you graduate? Who maintains this?"**
*   **Bad Answer:** "I'll do it in my free time."
*   **Winning Answer:** *"I am building a 'Handover Documentation' package. I can select 2 juniors from the 2nd/3rd year Computer Science department and train them as the new maintainers before I leave. This can be a prestigious club position."*

**Q: "Is student data safe?"**
*   **Winning Answer:** *"Yes. We use Google Firebase, the same industry-standard backend used by immense startup apps. We only store minimum necessary data (Year/Section) and do not track personal GPS locations permanently."*

**Q: "Why should we use this instead of the website/WhatsApp?"**
*   **Winning Answer:** *"The website is slow and not mobile-friendly. WhatsApp gets buried. This app sends **Notifications 15 mins before class**, works **Offline**, and has **Home Screen Widgets**. It ensures students never miss a class because they 'didn't check the group'."*

## 3. YOUR DEMO STRATEGY
When showing him the app:
1.  **Show the Widget First:** It's the "wow" factor. Show how it sits on the home screen.
2.  **Change the Theme:** Show him the Dark/Light mode. It shows polish.
3.  **Trigger a Notification:** "Sir, watch this." (Show the '15 minutes before' alert).
4.  **Offline Mode:** Turn off your WiFi/Data and show him the schedule still loads. *"It works even if campus WiFi is down."*
